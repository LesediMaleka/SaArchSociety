About


6 Degree Architects is a Johannesburg-based inter-disciplinary design studio, founded by Yusuf Patel and Adam Osman in 2020. Yusuf and Adam are two recent architecture graduates from the Graduate School of Architecture (GSA) at the University of Johannesburg.

 They are both invested in designing and enhancing emerging South African conditions at all levels of human social, cultural, and artistic endeavors, with an aim of developing a uniquely Johannesburg and African design language. Our firm is dedicated to architecture, urbanism, interior design and research.

Our clients and projects are diverse in nature and range from small home renovations to large inner-city developments as well as social outreach cases. We are currently partnered with Jewel City and are working on a project in Maboneng assisting artists in transforming the precinct with murals on the facades of multiple buildings.We are also involved in pro bono work and are currently designing a centre for abused women and children in the South of Johannesburg.

It is evident from the scale of projects that 6Degrees is involved with that they are a motivated team willing produce innovative solutions to new take on challenges.
